Greg Tech

GregTech… my favorite mod in Minecraft. It's not just about getting to the endgame or rushing through tiers—it's about the journey itself. Every step forward feels earned, from the first time you manage to smelt something without burning through your scarce resources, to the moment you finally automate a process that used to take ages by hand. GregTech teaches patience, planning, and problem-solving, and that's exactly what makes it so special. The mod isn't about the destination, but about the way there - each challenge shaping the experience and making the progress feel more meaningful than any quick win ever could.
