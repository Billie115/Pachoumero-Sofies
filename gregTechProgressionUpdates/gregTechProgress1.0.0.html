My Oxygen and Hydrogen Generator

So... this monstrosity, is my Oxygen Generator, mainly, but a byproduct on water electrolyzation in Hydrogen too, which never hearts to keep. REMINDER: KEEP OXYGEN AND HYDROGEN AWAY FROM EACH OTHER OR ELSE THEY GO BOOM... but that only happens IRL and not ingame, thankfully. It works by supplying the Basic Electolyzer with water and Low Voltage, and in return it gives me: Every 75 seconds, 2000L Oxygen and 1000L Hydrogen from 1000L water

My Large Bronze Boiler

This monster of a Boiler is my Pride and Joy. I've set it to 25% Throttle, and it produces 200L/t Steam. It curently supports up to 3 Steam Turbine, but at full Throttle it can support up to 12! I fuel it with Coke that I get from my Coke sey up!

Coke Ovens!

This is my very First, Automated set up in GregTech Community Pack. It works by auto inputing Coal from the Coke Oven Hatch and then outputing the Coke to the Spruce Drawer, and the Creosote to the Titanium Tanks on the right!

Basic Steam Generaos!

Well, we finaly reached my roots, where it all started... This little set up of 6 High Pressure Steam Solid Fuel Boilers And 6 High Pressure Steam Liquid Boiler, are the conrnerstone of my whole base... Well, before the Large Boiler that is, but still a solid start to my grand adventure...
